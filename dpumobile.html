dpümobile

mobile app for dumlupınar university.

introduction

the main purpose of the application is the access of students and academics to the university system.

as a result of the survey conducted among students and academicians, the most used processes in the university system were determined and the landing page was designed accordingly.

the pass



the pass can be used at the university turnstiles, public transportation, cafeterias and dormitory entrances. the pass system, on the other hand, eliminates the need for carrying more than one card and at the same time allows you to quickly complete all your transactions with your phone.
