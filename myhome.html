case study for my home

introduction

the main purpose of the "my home" company's website is to create a visually stunning and user-friendly platform that embodies modernity and minimalism. by adopting a sleek and clean design, we aim to capture the attention of users and leave a lasting impression that resonates with their sense of style.

landing page

the website carefully desinged with a focus on aesthetics and functionality, ensuring a seamless and intuitive browsing experience for potential customers. the minimalist approach will eliminate unnecessary clutter, allowing the products to take center stage and shine.



alternative desings



the pages are designed with 3 alternatives in order to increase the user experience and to see the alternative designs that can be used.

corporate website



the corporate website will serve as a comprehensive resource for customers, partners, and stakeholders, providing valuable information about the company's history, mission, and core values. through a visually appealing layout and engaging content, will communicate the brand's unique story and establish a strong connection with the target audience.
