bi' gün de

bi' gün de, the new way to travel.

introduction

bi' gün de is a mobile application designed to help tourists who do not know the city to visit which locations during touristic trips. the application presents the locations to be visited in the form of a route suggestion based on the preferences of users who have visited cities they have not been to before, so that tourists have the opportunity to make a more efficient trip.

the main aim of the app

the purpose of this application is to investigate the effect of destination image on city tourism and to design a daily route application by considering the results of revisiting, recommending, the most visited destinations etc. in the designed application, daily routes are created according to the preferences of the people, and if the user is satisfied with the route that he or she is on that day, a reconstructed route is suggested to the user by considering their preferences for the next day, and as a result of being satisfied with a day trip, this trip is turned into a few days or weekly trips. is the purpose.
